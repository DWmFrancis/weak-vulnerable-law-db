Presidential Records Act

Citation: 44 U.S. Code Chapter 22

Risk Score: 3 (Moderate)

Vulnerability Types: Enforcement Gaps, Ambiguity

Summary: Requires the preservation and public access to official records of the President and Vice President.

Historical Usage: Past administrations have had conflicts with compliance, including mishandling of documents.

Flagged Abuse Scenarios: Intentional destruction or concealment of documents to avoid accountability.

Suggested Reforms: Criminal penalties for non-compliance; clearer definitions of what constitutes a record.
