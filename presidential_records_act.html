Presidential Records Act

Citation

44 U.S. Code Chapter 22

Risk Score

3 (Medium)

Vulnerability Types

  • Enforcement Gaps
  • Norm Dependency

Summary

Requires preservation of official presidential records but has limited enforcement mechanisms.

Historical Usage

Violations often unpunished; disputes over classified material post-term.

Flagged Abuse Scenarios

Intentional destruction or removal of records to avoid transparency or accountability.

Suggested Reforms

Create enforcement authority and penalties; clarify standards for electronic records.
