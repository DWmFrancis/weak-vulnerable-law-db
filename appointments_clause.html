Appointments Clause

Citation

U.S. Constitution, Article II, Section 2, Clause 2

Risk Score

3 (Medium)

Vulnerability Types

  • Bypass Mechanisms
  • Ambiguity

Summary

Governs how federal officers are appointed; includes Senate confirmation for principal officers.

Historical Usage

Use of 'acting' appointments to avoid Senate oversight.

Flagged Abuse Scenarios

Presidents installing controversial figures in acting roles indefinitely.

Suggested Reforms

Limit duration of acting appointments; enforce Senate role in confirmations.
