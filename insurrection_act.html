Insurrection Act

Citation

10 U.S. Code §§ 251–255

Risk Score

5 (High)

Vulnerability Types

  • Ambiguity
  • Executive Overreach
  • Lack of Oversight

Summary

Grants the President authority to deploy military troops within the United States under specific circumstances without Congressional approval.

Historical Usage

Used in the 1992 LA riots; threatened in 2020 during nationwide protests.

Flagged Abuse Scenarios

A president using vague definitions of 'insurrection' to justify military force against political opponents or protests.

Suggested Reforms

Require congressional notification and approval within a short timeframe; clearer definitions of scope and limits.
