Hatch Act – 5 U.S. Code §§ 7321–7326

Status: Weak Enforcement

Risk Score:

3.9 / 5

Vulnerability Types:

  • Norm Dependency
  • Lack of Penalty

Summary:

Prohibits federal employees from political activity while on duty. However, violations are rarely punished, and enforcement is inconsistent.

Historical Use:

  • Numerous violations under Trump administration

Flagged Scenarios:

  • Use of federal office for campaign events

Suggested Reforms:

  • Mandatory fines
  • Clear enforcement timelines
