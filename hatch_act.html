Hatch Act

Citation: 5 U.S. Code §§ 7321–7326

Risk Score: 3 (Moderate)

Vulnerability Types: Weak Enforcement, Norm Dependency

Summary: Restricts federal employees from engaging in partisan political activity while on duty.

Historical Usage: Widely ignored or unenforced in recent administrations despite violations.

Flagged Abuse Scenarios: Use of official capacity or public resources for political gain without consequence.

Suggested Reforms: Add enforceable penalties; empower OSC with binding authority.
