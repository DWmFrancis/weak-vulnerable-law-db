Hatch Act

Citation

5 U.S. Code §§ 7321–7326

Risk Score

3 (Medium)

Vulnerability Types

  • Weak Penalties
  • Norm Dependency

Summary

Limits political activity by federal employees to maintain nonpartisan government.

Historical Usage

Numerous violations with few consequences.

Flagged Abuse Scenarios

Officials using federal office for campaigning without fear of penalty.

Suggested Reforms

Increase penalties and enforcement authority; establish independent review board.
