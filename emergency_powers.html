National Emergency Powers

Citation

50 U.S. Code § 1601 et seq.

Risk Score

5 (High)

Vulnerability Types

  • Overbroad Authority
  • Lack of Oversight

Summary

Grants the President extraordinary powers during declared emergencies with few time limits.

Historical Usage

Used to redirect military funds, impose sanctions, and more.

Flagged Abuse Scenarios

Declaring emergencies for political gain or bypassing normal legislative processes.

Suggested Reforms

Add automatic expiration unless renewed by Congress; require review panels.
