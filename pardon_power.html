Pardon Power – U.S. Constitution, Article II, Section 2

Status: Watchlist

Risk Score:

4.3 / 5

Vulnerability Types:

  • Unbounded Discretion
  • Conflict of Interest

Summary:

The pardon power is broad and unchecked. It can be used to protect allies or interfere in investigations without legal consequence.

Historical Use:

  • Pardons of political allies

Flagged Scenarios:

  • Pardoning co-conspirators

Suggested Reforms:

  • Ban self-pardons
  • Independent review body
