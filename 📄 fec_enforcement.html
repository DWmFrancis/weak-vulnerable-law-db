Campaign Finance / FEC Enforcement

Vulnerability:

Gridlock by design

Overview:

The Federal Election Commission is evenly split by party and lacks teeth. Major violations often go unpunished.

Exploited by:

SuperPACs, dark money groups, and candidates coordinating indirectly.

Key Weaknesses:

  • No timely enforcement
  • No consensus = no action
  • Anonymous donations laundered through shell orgs

Reform Ideas:

  • Reconfigure FEC for functional majority
  • Mandate real-time donor disclosure
  • Restrict dark money flows and require pass-through transparency
